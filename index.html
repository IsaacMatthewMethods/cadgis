About CADGIS

CADGIS is a leading provider of CAD and GIS solutions in Kaduna State, Nigeria. We specialize in delivering high-quality services for architectural designs, building construction, and land & property mapping.

With a team of experienced professionals, we are committed to meeting the diverse needs of our clients, from individuals to large corporations, across various industries. Our expertise ensures precision, efficiency, and innovative solutions for every project.

At CADGIS, we combine cutting-edge technology with industry best practices to deliver exceptional results. Our comprehensive services and tailored approach guarantee client satisfaction and project success.

  • Expertise in CAD for architectural and construction projects.
  • Accurate GIS solutions for land and property mapping.
  • Professional training programs for individuals and organizations.

CADGIS is dedicated to providing top-notch CAD and GIS solutions, tailored to meet the specific requirements of our clients. We are your trusted partner in achieving project success, with a focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Precision CAD Services for Architectural and Construction Excellence
